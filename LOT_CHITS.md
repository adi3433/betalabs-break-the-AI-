5. **Shuffle** before each team draws

- Each team picks **one chit**
- Once picked, **AI cannot be changed**
- After each team finishes, **return chit to box**
- Keep all 4 chits available for next teams

### Tips:

- Make multiple copies if running multiple sessions
- Laminate for durability (optional)
- Use a decorated box/bowl for drawing
- Create suspense during the drawing!
